As a project manager, I work with the group to define the scope of the project, set goals and objectives, and create a detailed project plan that outlines the tasks, timelines, and resources needed to complete the project. I then assign tasks based on each member's skills and interests, and set deadlines to ensure everyone is on track and the project stays on schedule.

I also monitor the progress of the project, track any issues or roadblocks that arise, and work with the team to come up with solutions to overcome them. Throughout the project, I communicate regularly with stakeholders including the client, faculty advisor, and other key individuals. I provide progress updates and ensure everyone is kept informed about the project's status.
